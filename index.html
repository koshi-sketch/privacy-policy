Information We Collect

We do not directly collect any personally identifiable information. However, the following third-party services used in the App may collect data:

Game progress, scores, and settings are stored locally on your device using the device's built-in storage. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional one-time purchase to remove advertisements. Purchase transactions are handled by Google Play and RevenueCat. We do not store your payment information.
